Honey Glazed Chicken

Honey Glazed Chicken is a sweet and savory dish coated in a delectable sticky glaze. The chicken is often prepared into bite-sized pieces heated over a large skillet. The glaze is filled with flavors of honey, soy sauce and ginger. It's often topped off with a stylish garnish.

The dish has a total time of about 40 to 60 minutes to make with preparation and cooking, and it depending on your ingredients it should yield about 4 servings. This makes it a great choice meal when you want something that doesn't take too long to make, and has a sizeable serving count.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
