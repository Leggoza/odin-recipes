Big Soft Ginger Cookies

Big Soft Ginger Cookies are an excellent treat if you're looking for a dessert with a little spice. This recipe in particular calls for cookies that will stay soft for days when stored in the right coniditions. So, one batch can last you for quite awhile.

The dish has a prep time of about 20 minutes, and a cook time of roughly 10 minutes. With any additional time you may need, the total time to make should be about 35 to 45 minutes. If you're looking for a savory delicious treat you can make in a pinch, look no further!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened

Steps
