Old-Fashioned Pancakes

If you need a perfect fluffy meal to start off your day, look no further than this old-fashioned pancake recipe. These pancakes are easy to make with ingredients you may already have in your kitchen, and making them from scratch will give them a flavor that beats any pre-made pancakes you can find.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and Butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter into a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.
  6. Serve with syrup and butter, and enjoy!
